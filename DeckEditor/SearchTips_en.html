In WEBXOSS, you can search card by its id, name, type, color, level, etc, combined.

Examples:

  • Card with ID WX01-001: WX01-001
  • Pre-built deck 02: WD02
  • White or red SIGNI: white red S
  • Level 0, blue, PR LRIG: blue 0 L pr
  • ARTS with use timing of attack phase: ARTS AttackPhase
  • Level 2 SIGNI with power 3000 or above: 3000+ 2
  • Tama only with life burst: tama burst
  • Tama only or unlimited, without life burst: tama+ burst-
  • <Earth Beast> SIGNI: EarthBeast
  • With [On-Play] and [Constant] abilities: OnPlay Constant
  • Illust by POP: Illust:POP
